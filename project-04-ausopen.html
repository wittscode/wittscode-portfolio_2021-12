25 Moments of the Australian Open

Celebrates the monumental moments of the Australian Open

To commemorate the long and storied history of the Australian Open, Tennis Australia and Monash University’s School of Media, Film & Journalism created a web-based documentary that is collecting and highlighting the most iconic moments throughout the centenary of the tournaments.

  • Tennis Australia
  • Storytelling, UI, Web Development
  • 2016

"We do not remember days, we remember moments."
- Cesare Pavese

From the legendary Rod Laver’s feat in the inhospitable weather during the foundation days to the grueling match between modern-day household names such as Roger Federer and Rafael Nadal.

The web documentary aims to capture the spirits and the magical moments of the what the Australian Open is.

The stories of trials, tribulations and triumph that is happened under the heat of the Australian’s summer.
