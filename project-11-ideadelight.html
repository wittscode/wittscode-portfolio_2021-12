Idea Delight

Branding and Website for an Immersive Multimedia Studio

Idea Delight is a veteran multimedia production studio store based in the suburb of Bangkok. With 10+ years of experience and wide offerings of technical and creative developments throughout Asia, the company sought to improve their online presence and reaches. I developed the visual style for the brand as well as architecting a plan for the company’s online presence. I designed a site map, representative desktop and mobile landing pages based on the brand identity and the company’s offerings.
